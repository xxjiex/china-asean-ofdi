# China–ASEAN OFDI and Economic Growth

## 1. Research Question
- MAIN: Does China’s OFDI in ASEAN promote economic growth in host countries?
- MECHANISM: Is the effect moderated (or mediated) by host-country infrastructure development?
- EXTRA: Decarbonisation Effect of OFDI

## 2. Contribution
- Mechanism analysis
- Policy implications for gov and enterprises

## 3. Data
- World Bank: GDP per capita, controls
- Statistical Bulletin of China's OFDI: OFDI stock
- Period: 2003–2023

## 4. Methodology
- Panel, two-way fixed effects
- Instrumental variables using lagged OFDI

## 5. Results
- OFDI has a positive and statistically significant effect on GDP growth.
- Higher levels of urbanization and infrastructure weaken the short-term growth effect of China–ASEAN OFDI
- China’s OFDI significantly reduces ASEAN countries’ carbon emissions.

## 6. Skills Demonstrated
- Panel data modeling
- Endogeneity handling
- Economic interpretation
